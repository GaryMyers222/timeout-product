# TimeOut Vision

TimeOut helps trusted friends trade babysitting easily.

The system coordinates sit requests among a small circle of nearby parents so that families can enjoy occasional TimeOut while maintaining trust and reciprocity.

Core Principles:

- Trusted friends, not strangers
- Invitation-only circles
- Reciprocity balanced through points
- Minimal friction for busy parents
- Coordination primarily through phones

The goal is circles that can last for decades and become neighborhood assets.
